About Apollo Cinema

A long time ago in a galaxy far far away.... Apollo Cinema was founded, it started with a small idea among men and then grew into something amazing. We first decided to create one cinema in Manchester but popularity pushed us to expand our branch opening two more cinemas across the United Kingdom in London and Sheffield.

We offer a range of viewings which include 2D,3D and newly fitted 4D screenings. As our aim is to give our customers the most enjoyable and luxurios experience with Apollo Cinema, accommodating for families with children of all ages.
